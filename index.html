About Me

Passionate Storyteller & Change Maker

Hi! I'm Suhani Bisht, popularly known as "Honey Girl" in the digital world. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor of Journalism & Mass Communication at Graphic Era University, where I'm honing my skills to become a powerful voice in media.

My journey began with a simple desire to make a difference. During my school days, I used my reporting skills to bring attention to my village's road issues, which ultimately led to the road being rebuilt - a small victory that showed me the power of storytelling and advocacy.

Today, I balance my academic pursuits with a growing social media presence, where I create trending lifestyle and fashion content while fearlessly speaking out on important social issues. My Instagram community of 50K+ followers and growing YouTube channel "Honeygirl05" are platforms where I share authentic stories and connect with people who believe in the power of truth.
